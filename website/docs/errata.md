## P.50 Equation (3.3)

$$
h(x) = \left\{
\begin{array}{ll}
\text{mix}(0.3, 0.9, c(x+1)) = 0.9 + 1.8x^2 + 1.2x^3 & (-1 \leq x \leq 0)\\
\text{mix}(0.9, 0.6, c(x)) = 0.9 - 0.9x^2 + 0.6x^3 & (0 \leq x \leq 1)
\end{array}
\right.
$$

$$
h(x) = \left\{
\begin{array}{ll}

\text{mix}(0.9, 0.6, c(x)) = 0.9 - 0.9x^2 + 0.6x^3 & (0 \leq x \leq 1)
\end{array}
\right.
$$
